# Mech-Zilla

#### Aspects

The Lord of Lizards, Mechanical Behemoth, The Roaring Titan, Tokyo's Apocalypse

#### Concepts

Destructive Force: When Mech-Zilla uses an Overcome, destroy a component in this zone

#### Skills

Endurance 6, Might 5, Intimidation 4, Strike 3, Alertness 2, Conviction 1.

#### Unique (**Flame Breath**)

Aoe DC 9 Shoot or take 1. Create Concept "X is burning".

#### Routine (**Destroy Tokyo**)

R1: Tail Swipe: Aoe DC 9 or take 2.  
R2: Topple: Destroy all Concepts and Components in this zone
Ult: Collapse: This zone and all adjacent zones. DC 12 or take 4.

#### Vanquish

V1: Use Flame Breath.
V2: Disable Destroy Tokyo
V3: Disable Flame Breath
V4: Use Tail Swipe
